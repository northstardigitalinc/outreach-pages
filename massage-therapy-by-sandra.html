About

Sandra Borg-Sandhu, RMT

Sandra has been practicing registered massage therapy in Brampton for over a decade. Her solo practice runs almost entirely on referrals — clients send their mothers, their partners, their co-workers. Many of them found Sandra during high-risk pregnancies and never left.

She brings a holistic touch to every session: treating the whole person, not just the complaint. Whether it's chronic low-back pain, prenatal discomfort, or stress that's built up over months, Sandra's care is both precise and deeply reassuring.
