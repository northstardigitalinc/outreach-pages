About Catherine

Catherine is a rare dual-credentialed practitioner: a Certified Athletic Trainer and Licensed Massage Therapist who previously worked in athletic training at Alliance Community Hospital.

That sports-medicine background shapes every session, from structural integration and neuromuscular work to sports and deep tissue massage. She treats a wide range, from athletes to pregnancy and geriatric clients.
