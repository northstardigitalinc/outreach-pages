About

Cheyenne (Chey) Threlkeld, RMT

Chey graduated from the West Coast College of Massage Therapy in 2018 and has spent the years since building a Kitsilano practice grounded in honest, individualized care. She sees a wide range of clients — pregnant women, athletes, infants, and anyone who needs genuine relief from tension that's accumulated over time.

Her clinical reasoning is strong, but what clients notice first is how much she communicates. Before anything else, she wants to know what you're experiencing, what hasn't worked before, and what pressure feels right to you. That approach is why 100% of her reviewed clients recommend her.
