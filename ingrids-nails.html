Questions About Booking with Ingrid

How do I book an appointment?

Call or text Ingrid directly at 615-873-4320, or reach out through her Facebook or Instagram pages. She's responsive and will help you find a time that works. Appointments fill quickly, so don't wait too long.

Is it really appointment only?

Yes — Ingrid works exclusively by appointment. No walk-ins, no waiting room. This is what makes the experience so different: when you arrive, Ingrid is ready for you and only you. Your appointment gets her full focus from start to finish.

How far in advance should I book?

Ingrid recommends reaching out at least 1–2 weeks ahead, and even further for special occasions, events, or detailed nail art. Her schedule stays full — loyal clients book their next appointment before they leave. The sooner you reach out, the better.

What services does Ingrid offer?

Ingrid offers manicures, pedicures, acrylic sets, gel nails, and custom nail art. Every service is tailored to your nail type, style preferences, and lifestyle. If you're not sure what's right for you, just ask — she'll guide you to the best option.

Where is Ingrid located?

Ingrid works out of her studio at 433 Cotton Lane, Nashville, TN. Exact location details are shared when you book your appointment.
