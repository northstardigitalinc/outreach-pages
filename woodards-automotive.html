Frequently Asked Questions

Do you give me an estimate before starting the work?

Always. We'll tell you what's wrong and what it'll cost before anyone touches your car. No surprise charges on your bill — that's just how we do things.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Give us a call at 615-859-6163 and we'll get you scheduled. We recommend calling ahead so we can set aside time for you, but we'll do our best to work you in same-day if something comes up.

What kinds of vehicles do you work on?

Most makes and models — domestic and foreign. Cars, trucks, SUVs. If you're not sure whether we can help, just call Joe and ask. We'll give you a straight answer.

Will you try to sell me services I didn't come in for?

No. That's actually what people say about us more than anything else — we don't upsell. If your car doesn't need it, we'll tell you that. If something else catches our eye during the inspection, we'll point it out so you can decide. But we won't pressure you.
